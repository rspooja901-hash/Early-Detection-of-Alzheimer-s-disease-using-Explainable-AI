# Exploration of Explainable AI for Early Detection of Alzheimer’s Disease
## Problem Statement
Early detection of Alzheimer’s disease is challenging due to limited specialists and late diagnosis, especially in public healthcare systems.
## Proposed Solution
An AI-based system that uses deep learning to analyze MRI brain images and explainable AI to provide transparent and trustworthy predictions for early Alzheimer’s detection.
## Key Features
- Early disease detection
- Deep learning-based MRI analysis
- Explainable AI visual outputs
- Doctor-friendly dashboard
## Technology Stack
- Python
- TensorFlow / PyTorch
- CNN
- LIME / SHAP
- Flask
## System Requirements
- CPU/GPU-enabled system
- Minimum 8GB RAM
- Internet connection (optional)
## Target Users
- Public hospitals
- Doctors and healthcare workers
- Community healthcare centers
## Impact
Improves early diagnosis, reduces healthcare burden, and enhances access to medical support for communities.
